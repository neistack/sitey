About Us

Jaskaran Pharmaha, commonly known by his family, friends, and professional colleagues as Jessy, is a licensed lawyer in Ontario and is the founder of Jaskaran Pharmaha Professional Corporation. He is a results-driven, business-savvy, and passionate barrister and solicitor prepared to assist his clients with Real Estate Law transactions and Criminal Law matters in Ontario. 

Jaskaran Pharmaha earned his law degree (LL.B.) in the United Kingdom and is a proud alumnus of the University of Leicester, School of Law. Jaskaran completed the Law Practice Program (LPP) at Toronto Metropolitan University, where he completed a four-month law practice training course and four months of in person law practice work experience. Furthermore, Jaskaran completed his law practice work experience during the LPP at a well-known law chamber in downtown Toronto, working for two criminal defence lawyers, where he gained invaluable experience in criminal law.

He previously worked at Legal Aid Ontario CLSC and has a demonstrated ability to exercise sound judgment when dealing with clients, lawyers, and various agencies.

Jaskaran Pharmaha is a member of the Law Society of Ontario, and he is a member of the Criminal Lawyers’ Association. He is pleased to offer his legal services to the community in English, French, Punjabi, and Hindi.
