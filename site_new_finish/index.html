Navigating Legal Landscapes:
Real Estate Lawyer & Criminal Defence Lawyer

Jaskaran Pharmaha Professional Corporation is a sole practice law firm located in the North-GTA with easy access from Highways 400 and 407, Steeles Avenue West, and Weston Road. Our main areas of practice are Real Estate Law and Criminal Law. Our firm believes in providing effective legal counsel for clients with the goal of attaining meaningful results to help people move forward in a positive way.  We possess a unique business acumen and strongly believe in the importance of providing excellent customer service through a personalized approach. Whether you’re navigating a significant event such as a residential or commercial real estate transaction or facing challenging circumstances like being charged with a criminal offence, our firm is here to assist you.
